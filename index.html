Interactive Standard Operating Procedure

Important Notice

This SOP is mandatory for all team members and vendors. Please read carefully and follow every section to ensure excellence in our events.

Team & Roles

Project Manager (PM)

Leads the project lifecycle from booking to final execution, coordinates all team and vendors.

Meeting Manager (MM)

Conducts pre-wedding client meetings, finalizes details, updates the Wedding Report.

Client Manager (CM)

Assists the couple during the event, ensures Photography & Videography meet standards.

Area Manager (AM)

Responsible for specific event zones, ensuring vendor performance and quality standards.

Head Manager / Finance / Development

Oversees management, finance, and vendor approval processes.

Core Principles

Venue Cleanliness

Ceremony areas must be spotless, politely ask bystanders to move away during ceremonies.

Follow the Rundown

Strict adherence to the event timeline is required, report delays immediately.

Client Arrival

Client must arrive only when all preparations are 100% complete.

Vendor Punctuality

Vendors must arrive on time, otherwise, payment is withheld.

Approved Vendors Only

Only officially listed vendors may be used unless approved by management.

Respect Client Orders

All services must match exactly what was ordered and confirmed in writing.

Event Lifecycle

Vendor Booking

All bookings via official company email only. Confirmation is mandatory.

Rundown Preparation

Timeline must be finalized well before the wedding, depending on size.

Technical Meetings

Mandatory pre-event meetings for key vendors, rehearsals for the couple.

Client Updates

All last-minute client changes must be communicated to vendors promptly.

Client Briefing

Provide a short, clear briefing to the couple upon arrival.

Atmosphere & Music

Music must be played during guest arrival; microphones ready for ceremony.

Vendor & Team Standards

Appearance

Professional uniform required for all staff; vendors must wear black attire.

Behavior & Conduct

No alcohol, no loud talking, personal mobile phone use prohibited on-site.

Anti-Fraud Policy

Side deals are forbidden. All payments go through official channels only.

Admin & Emergency Contacts

Vendor Management

All new vendors require formal approval; team members cannot act as vendors.

Sanctions

3-strike system for internal staff; vendor violations affect future collaboration.

Emergency Contacts

Urgent issues must be escalated to management. Head: head@7.agency, Finance: finance@thesevenagency.com
