Team & Roles

Project Manager (PM): Full responsibility for event execution, from booking to delivery.
Meeting Manager (MM): Organizes pre-wedding meetings, communicates logistics with client.
Client Manager (Butler): Supports couple throughout the day, assists photography team.
Operations Coordinator: Handles setup, technical checks, vendor arrival.

Core Principles

Venues must be clean, private, and properly prepared before client arrival.
All team members follow the official event rundown strictly.
Clients only arrive after management confirms full readiness.
Communication with the couple is always professional and solution-oriented.

Event Lifecycle

PM assigned after deposit payment. All vendor bookings via official email.
Event rundown draft created and reviewed one month prior.

Vendor & Team Standards

Professional uniforms mandatory during event hours.
Discretion required — no personal phones visible.
Zero tolerance for private deals, bribery, or fraud.
All vendors must pass quality checks; new vendors need approval.

Administration & Contacts

New vendor applications go through management verification.
In emergencies, contact head@7.agency or senior management directly.
All SOP updates announced via internal newsletter.
