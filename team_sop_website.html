Team Roles & Responsibilities

Project Manager (PM)

Leads the event, oversees bookings, manages the rundown, and ensures standards are met.

Client Manager (CM)

Personal assistant to the couple, manages their schedule, and ensures a calm experience.

Area Manager (AM)

Oversees specific event areas, ensures vendor quality, and reports to the PM.

Vendor Partnership Standards

Vendors are essential to our success. These guidelines ensure flawless events together.

Photography & Videography

  • Arrive 15 minutes early
  • Mandatory shot lists provided
  • English communication with clients
  • Deliverables: Photos in 2 weeks, Video in 4 weeks

Florals, Decor & Furniture

  • All setup completed 15 minutes prior
  • Flawless execution per approved design
  • Fresh flowers, clean furniture

Catering & Bar

  • Accurate menu & beverage service
  • Professional appearance of all staff
  • Spotless hygiene standards

Entertainment & Other Vendors

  • Setup complete before performance
  • Approved setlists and attire
  • Special effect safety is mandatory
